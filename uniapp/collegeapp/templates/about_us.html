About Us

Envision College is a premier educational institution dedicated to providing students with the knowledge, skills, and values necessary to succeed in a dynamic global society. Founded in 2005, we have been committed to fostering a diverse and inclusive learning environment where students can explore their passions, develop their talents, and prepare for their future careers.

Our highly qualified faculty is passionate about their fields and dedicated to providing students with an engaging and personalized learning experience. We offer a wide range of undergraduate and graduate programs, including Business Administration, Computer Science, Engineering, Education. Our programs are designed to meet the needs of the modern workforce and prepare students for successful careers in their chosen fields.

Envision College is accredited by the Accrediting Council for Business Schools and Programs (ACBSP) and the Commission on Colleges of the Southern Association of Colleges and Schools (SACS). We are proud to be a member of the Association of Colleges and Universities of the West (ACUW).
